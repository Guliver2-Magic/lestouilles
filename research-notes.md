## Best Catering Website Design Trends

### Key Design Elements from Top Catering Sites:

1. **Color Palettes**:
   - Black, white, and cream for luxury/elegance
   - Bright accent colors (lime green, orange, red) for vibrancy
   - Seafoam green and dark gray-green for modern sophistication

2. **Visual Elements**:
   - Large, striking hero images
   - High-quality food photography
   - Image sliders/carousels on homepage
   - Video integration to showcase services
   - Photo galleries for culinary creations

3. **Typography**:
   - Mix of font weights (thick, thin, caps, lowercase)
   - Creative, decorative fonts for brand identity
   - Clear, readable menu fonts

4. **Layout & Structure**:
   - Clean, uncluttered layouts
   - Strategic use of white space
   - Accessible navigation bars
   - Prominent CTA buttons
   - Background textures for depth

5. **Key Features**:
   - Awards and testimonials prominently displayed
   - Easy-to-navigate menu sections
   - Contact forms with event planning fields
   - Press/media pages
   - Gallery pages

### Parallax & Video Trends:
- Video backgrounds in hero sections
- Parallax scrolling effects for depth
- Smooth transitions between sections
- Full-screen video showcasing food preparation
